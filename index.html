GOFUNDME TEACHERS CAMPAIGN

Spread the value of well-being through yoga with us!

f you are a teacher and wish to start or expand your audience and spread the joy of yoga, then we have something very exciting for you. Join us in our endeavour to organise and execute Isha yoga practices accross places in and your own.

Initiative

We are trying to partner with Isha Yoga teachers and volunteers to organise and execute Isha yoga classes in yoga studios and other places. We offer to sponsor the programs costs.

Our Ask

We want teachers and volunteers just like you, to help us execute this initiative. So, if you are willing, please reach out to us by filling out the form below.

Support

We appreciate your support for this initiative. If you wish to add to this cause, then please donate whatever you can to bear the costs incurred in organising this initiative.

Spread the word

If you see the value in this initiative for yourself, then we fully encourage you to spread the word about this cause and bring in more teachers, volunteers and students just like yourself, so we may continue to organise these programs in the future.
